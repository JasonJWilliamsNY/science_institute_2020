07 PCR Basics

Important terms/concepts

  • Exponential curve (or growth)
  • Chain reaction
  • Taq Polymerase
  • PCR primers
  • Electrophoresis (of PCR product)

Lecture

Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) is one of the most important techniques in the molecular biology toolkit. PCR allows us to replicate a specific piece of DNA in the genome. Replicating a piece of DNA is the first step to analyzing it (via DNA sequencing). Replicated DNA is also often one of the first steps to editing DNA for subsequent experiments.
